1 What is Generative AI
Generative AI refers to a class of artificial intelligence models that can create new content ‚Äî such as text, images, audio, code, or video ‚Äî that resembles human-created data.

Examples

LLM apps like ChatGPT Diffusion models for images Code-gen LLMs like CodeLlama TTS models like ElevenLabs Video gen models like Sora
2 Generative AI vs Traditional AI
Traditional AI Generative AI
Finds patterns in data and gives predictions Learns the distribution of data so it can generate a new sample from it
Examples: classification, regression, detection Can mimic humans ‚Äî creates original text, images, code, audio, video

7 Chatbot 4 ‚Äî Agentic AI
üß† Agentic AI  |  Proactive ¬∑ Adaptive ¬∑ Autonomous

Key difference ‚Äî one prompt triggers the entire workflow

I want to hire a backend engineer.
‚úÖ Goal: Hire a remote backend engineer (2‚Äì4 yrs experience)

Proposed plan:
1. Draft JD ‚Üí post on best platforms
2. Monitor pipeline ‚Üí adjust strategy if needed
3. Screen candidates ‚Üí schedule interviews
4. Draft + send offer ‚Üí start onboarding

Agentic behaviour at each stage

JD DraftingProactively drafts JD using company documents. Presents it: "Do you want me to make changes?" ‚Üí User: "No, this is fine."
Job PostingSuggests platforms, gets approval, posts via API ‚Üí "Job posted. I will monitor the applications and keep you posted." Monitors autonomously.
Revising JDDetects only 2 applications ‚Äî below expectations. Proactively alerts: suggests broadening JD to full-stack + promote on LinkedIn. Gets approval ‚Üí "Revised JD posted. Promotions activated. I'll continue monitoring."
SchedulingChecks calendar ‚Üí "You are free this Friday. Do you want me to schedule the interview?" ‚Üí User confirms ‚Üí invitation drafted + sent.
InterviewingProactively sends reminder: "Quick reminder! You have 2 interviews today." Also emails a document of past interview questions for the role.
Offer LetterDrafts offer ‚Üí user approves ‚Üí "Offer letter sent. Tracking acceptance."
OnboardingOn acceptance: welcome email sent, IT access submitted, laptop provisioned. Then: "Shall I schedule intro meetings?" ‚Üí Yes ‚Üí scheduled.

‚úÖ All problems solved

  • ‚úì Proactive ‚Äî initiates actions, monitors, sends reminders unprompted
  • ‚úì Memory ‚Äî maintains full context across entire workflow
  • ‚úì Specific Advice ‚Äî uses company knowledge base
  • ‚úì Takes Actions ‚Äî posts, emails, schedules, onboards via APIs
  • ‚úì Adapts ‚Äî detects low applications, revises strategy autonomously
8 Conclusion & Evolution Summary
Purpose Gen AI is about creating content.
Agentic AI is about solving a goal.
Behaviour Gen AI is reactive ‚Äî waits for prompts.
Agentic AI is proactive & autonomous.
Relationship Generative AI is a building block of Agentic AI ‚Äî agentic systems use LLMs for reasoning and generation, but add memory, tools, and the ability to plan and act autonomously.
